# Before You Begin – Client Onboarding

Before onboarding to re:Search, CMS vendors and courts must complete several foundational steps.  
This ensures the technical and operational prerequisites are in place before integration begins.

---

## Prerequisites

### Business Requirements  
- Active agreement for re:Search participation  
- Identified stakeholder contacts (PM, technical lead, court SME)  
- Clear integration scope (case types, courts, markets)

### Technical Requirements  
- CMS must support one of the approved integration modes:  
  - Batch  
  - ECF  
  - Non-Integrated (limited)  
- Ability to generate required schemas and transport  
- Ability to support authentication requirements (mTLS or S3)

### Operational Requirements  
- Access to test environment(s)  
- Internal support team identified  
- Logging, error tracking, and monitoring processes established
